Assembler/Assembly Technician

Aleph Objects, Inc. has multiple spots available for the position of Assembler. Major job duties will include:

  1. Assemble fabricated parts at floor stations.
  2. Uses hand tools and power tools to assemble units according to product specifications.
  3. Test and calibrate parts and mechanisms to meet tolerances and product specifications.
  4. Identify units that fail tests or tolerance levels and repair as necessary.
  5. Rely on instructions and pre-established guidelines to perform the functions of the job.
  6. Flexible and can perform a variety of tasks.

This position requires a high school diploma or its equivalent. May be required to complete an apprenticeship and/or formal training in area of specialty. Prefers 2-5 years of industry experience.

Manufacturing Engineer

Aleph Objects, Inc. is currently accepting applications for the full time position: Manufacturing Engineer.


Requirements:

  1. Engineering Degree in pertinent field
  2. Significant experience as a Manufacturing Engineer
  3. Experience with document control
  4. Experience with manufacturing tooling like test equipment, crimpers and hand tools
  5. Experience with Quality control

Duties:

  1. Support manufacturing
  2. Get trained, understand, and implement new design or design changes generated by R&D into manufacturing
  3. Document new products for manufacturing and update as changes occur
  4. Design/Build/Purchase fixturing for manufacturing
  5. Actively work with R&D to resolve design-related, process-related (as recommended by R&D), or material related issues that might have an impact on product quality/reliability or production output including performing failure analysis and design of experiment when need be.
  6. Oversee Quality control in manufacturing
  7. Review processes and procedures for efficiency
  8. Provide feedback and suggestions to R&D to improve reliability or enhance the manufacturability of the product
  9. Be an active member and take part in the new product launch process. Provide manufacturing engineering readiness plan with actions and targets to timely support the new product launch.
  10. Install/implement, maintain and provide training on all manufacturing equipment. Initiate requests to R&D and collaborate with R&D on design and implementation of custom in-house built tools and test equipment.

Please send your resume to jobs@alephobjects.com.


Technical Support Representative

Aleph Objects, Inc. is searching for a full time Technical Support Representative. A technical support representatives job will entail assisting customer issues with mechanical and software challenges that may occur during the operation of a 3D printer. It is important to help the customer in a manner that is polite and friendly, while anticipating any future issues that may arise. Patience is a must, as much of your job will be teaching customers how to use a new and exciting piece of technology. You must by willing to use Free Software, Libre Innovation, and Open Hardware (FLO) for all responsibilities required for this position. This position can require evening and/or weekend hours.


Role and Responsibilities:

  1. Answer incoming questions via phone and email in a friendly informative manner.
  2. Responsible for troubleshooting and diagnostics through process of elimination.
  3. Aleph Objects Inc. releases early and releases often. One must be willing to experiment and test new products. Providing feedback for streamlining the processes and increasing efficiency for the tech support department is imperative.
  4. Aleph Objects Inc. is a 100% FLO company. A willingness to use 100% open source hardware and software is essential.
  5. Ability to follow established practices and procedures.
  6. Responsible for Customer returns and exchanges. Ability to efficiently set up, process, and ensure delivery of items to appropriate departments and or locations.
  7. Responsible for maintaining a professional attitude and workplace demeanor at all times.
  8. Ability to work with customers of all technical levels. LulzBot supplies printers to a wide variety of customers, and the ability to relate to all levels is required.
  9. Prompt, consistent attendance is required for this position.
  10. Maintaining our customers high expectations of Technical Support is essential to our thriving company.

Skills and Specifications:

  1. Strong personal and interdepartmental communications skills.
  2. A strong desire to impart knowledge and suggestions to those less experienced.
  3. Knowledge of manufacturing materials equipment and supplies.
  4. Ability to prioritize issues and follow through with commitments.
  5. Ability to recognize and diffuse tense situations, whether personally or through escalation..
  6. Demonstrate ability to solve problems, analyze symptoms, and suggest appropriate solutions through training and individual experience.
  7. Strong attention to detail.

Education and or Experience Requirements:

  1. High School Diploma or equivalent is required.
  2. One to two years experience in customer or technical support is desired.
  3. Experience with 3D printers, specifically LulzBot and RepRap hardware printers and hardware, is highly desired.

3D Printer Fleet technician

Aleph Objects, Inc. is searching for a full time, 3D printer fleet technician. Job responsibilities will include running a fleet of 40+ 3D printers, repairing and maintaining fleet printers, part production planning, and part processing and inspection.


Experience with 3D printers is highly desired but not required. General knowledge of mechanical equipment is a plus. Basic computer skills are required and experience with GNU/Linux is a plus. Time management is a MAJOR factor of this position including managing part production quotas, timelines, and deadlines.


The LulzBot fleet runs 24 hours a day; day, evening, or night shifts are a possibility. This position requires standing the majority of the work day. On occasion this position requires lifting 30-40 lb packages.


Please send your resume to jobs@alephobjects.com. Any other relevant work or research on 3D printers such as blogs or documentation that exemplifies your experience and knowledge of 3D printers is also welcome. Wage will be determined by experience.
