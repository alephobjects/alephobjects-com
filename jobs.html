3D Printer Technician

Aleph Objects, Inc. is searching for a full time, 3D printer technician. Job responsibilities will include running a cluster of 100+ 3D printers, repairing and maintaining 3D printers, part production planning, and part processing and inspection.


Experience with 3D printers is highly desired but not required. General knowledge of mechanical equipment is a plus. Basic computer skills are required and experience with GNU/Linux is a plus. Time management is a MAJOR factor of this position including managing part production quotas, timelines, and deadlines.


Role and Responsibilities:

  1. Operation of 3D Printers in a production setting
  2. Repair and Maintenance of 3D Printers
  3. Adhering to and managing of printing production quotas and deadlines
  4. Cleaning of 3D prints for use in the production process
  5. Inspecting prints to assure adherence to quality expectations
  6. Preparing files for use in 3D printing
  7. Consistent attention to production quality
  8. Prompt and consistent attendance is required for this position
  9. And other duties as assigned

Work Experience:

  1. No minimum requirements
  2. 3D printer experience is a plus
  3. GNU/Linux is a plus

Skills, Certifications, and Education:

  1. Written & Verbal Communication
  2. 3D printer experience is a plus
  3. GNU/Linux is a plus

Day, evening, or night shifts are available. This position requires standing the majority of the work day. On occasion this position requires lifting 30-40 lb. packages.

Assembler/Assembly Technician

Aleph Objects, Inc. has multiple spots available for the position of Assembler. Major job duties will include:

  1. Assemble fabricated parts at floor stations.
  2. Uses hand tools and power tools to assemble units according to product specifications.
  3. Test and calibrate parts and mechanisms to meet tolerances and product specifications.
  4. Identify units that fail tests or tolerance levels and repair as necessary.
  5. Rely on instructions and pre-established guidelines to perform the functions of the job.
  6. Flexible and can perform a variety of tasks.

This position requires a high school diploma or its equivalent. May be required to complete an apprenticeship and/or formal training in area of specialty. Prefers 2-5 years of industry experience.

Mechanical Engineer

Aleph Objects, Inc.® is currently accepting applications for the full time position: Mechanical Engineer.


Designs, develops, and tests all aspects of mechanical systems, components and materials related to 3D printing. Applies knowledge of engineering principles to design products such as print heads, printer components and system level 3D printers. May be involved in process design, fixturing for assembly and failure analysis of 3D printing products. Familiar with standard practices and procedures relating to working within an Open Source environment, including correct licensing of products and use of strictly Free Software and Open Source Hardware whenever possible. Relies on experience and judgment to plan and accomplish goals. May be required to perform analysis of new 3D printing materials, as well as profiling materials for printing across a range of printers. Works under general supervision, a certain degree of creativity and latitude is required. This position consists of approximately 50% sustaining engineering of existing products, and 50% new product development.


Roles and Responsibilities

  1. Assessing project requirements
  2. Agreeing to timescales and specifications with managers
  3. Working with suppliers on technical details
  4. Problem solving
  5. Undertaking relevant research
  6. Producing and implementing designs and test procedures
  7. Measuring performance of mechanical components and devices
  8. Testing, evaluating, modifying and re-testing products
  9. Writing reports and documentation
  10. Providing technical advice
  11. Analyzing and interpreting data
  12. Build prototypes, products, and systems for testing
  13. Supports firmware development
  14. Prompt consistent attendance is required for this position
  15. And other duties as assigned

Work Experience

  1. Minimum of 2 - 5 years relevant experience
  2. 3D printer experience is a plus

Skills, Certifications, and Education

  1. Bachelor's degree in relevant engineering discipline minimum.
  2. Written and verbal communication
  3. Computer skills
  4. Technical skills
