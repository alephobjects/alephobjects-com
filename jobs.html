Digital Media Production Artist

Aleph Objects, Inc.® is currently accepting applications for the full time position: Digital Media Production Artist.


Our ideal candidate has a portfolio demonstrating excellent visual design skills and an understanding that creative output has enormous influence on brand perception. This individual knows that outstanding visual design is not dependent on the use of specific software tools. Aleph Objects is dedicated to Free Software and we utilize tools like GNU Image Manipulation Program, Inkscape, and Scribus, so a willingness to learn and an ability to quickly adapt to new creative software is a must. This position will also involve capturing and editing digital photos and videos, so experience with digital photo/video equipment and lighting is necessary.


Output to include product and instructional photos and videos, design/layout of printed documentation and sales collateral, product packaging design, digital advertisements, and more. Works under general supervision of Creative Manager, but must be able to work independently, prioritizing projects to meet department production schedule deadlines.


Roles and Responsibilities

    Primary

    • Product, people, and event photography and videography including post-processing and editing
    • Organization of digital photo/video/audio assets
    • Graphic design and layout for print and web
    • Packaging design
    • Website and social media visual asset management

    Secondary

    • Motion design and animation
    • 3D model design
    • 3D animation

Creative Software Utilized: GNU Image Manipulation Program, Inkscape, Scribus, Synfig Studio, OpenShot, Kdenlive, Audacity, Darktable, Natron, Blender


Work Experience

  • Minimum of 1-2 years relevant experience
  • 3D printer experience is a plus
  • Experience with Free Software creative tools is also a plus

Skills, Certifications, and Education

  • Bachelor's degree in relevant discipline preferred
  • Excellent visual design and composition skills
  • Competency with photo and video equipment
  • Excellent written and verbal communication
  • High level of computer competency. Experience with GNU/Linux is a plus

Working Conditions/Physical Effort

  • This is an on-site position and remote workers are not being considered.
  • Compensation for relocation is not available for this position.
  • No visa sponsorship is available for this position.
