3D Printer Technician

Experience with 3D printers is highly desired but not required. General knowledge of mechanical equipment is a plus. Basic computer skills are required and experience with GNU/Linux is a plus. Time management is a MAJOR factor of this position including managing part production quotas, timelines, and deadlines.


Role and Responsibilities:

  1. Operation of 3D Printers in a production setting
  2. Repair and Maintenance of 3D Printers
  3. Adhering to and managing of printing production quotas and deadlines
  4. Cleaning of 3D prints for use in the production process
  5. Inspecting prints to assure adherence to quality expectations
  6. Preparing files for use in 3D printing
  7. Consistent attention to production quality
  8. Prompt and consistent attendance is required for this position
  9. And other duties as assigned

Work Experience:

  1. No minimum requirements
  2. 3D printer experience is a plus
  3. GNU/Linux is a plus

Skills, Certifications, and Education:

  1. Written & Verbal Communication
  2. 3D printer experience is a plus
  3. GNU/Linux is a plus

Day, evening, or night shifts are available. This position requires standing the majority of the work day. On occasion this position requires lifting 30-40 lb. packages.

Assembler/Assembly Technician

Aleph Objects, Inc. has multiple spots available for the position of Assembler. Major job duties will include:

  1. Assemble fabricated parts at floor stations.
  2. Uses hand tools and power tools to assemble units according to product specifications.
  3. Test and calibrate parts and mechanisms to meet tolerances and product specifications.
  4. Identify units that fail tests or tolerance levels and repair as necessary.
  5. Rely on instructions and pre-established guidelines to perform the functions of the job.
  6. Flexible and can perform a variety of tasks.

Shipping and Receiving Clerk

Aleph Objects, Inc. is currently accepting applications for the full time position: Shipping and Receiving Clerk. This role performs order fulfillment of products and parts from warehouse to customers and distribution centers. Manage receiving of goods safely and accurately. Quickly and accurately record movement of inventory into ERP software.


Responsibilities will include:

  1. Accurately ship domestic, international, or LTL freight outgoing shipments including small boxes to large pallets.
  2. Ensure proper packaging/handling of parts, pick and pack, and labeling. Build and break down pallet shipments up to 7ft tall.
  3. Unload incoming shipments, safely and accurately manage receiving and order fulfillment of parts and products into manufacturing, warehouse, or distribution locations.
  4. Maintain system transactions and paperwork to validate the processing of material through the ERP warehouse management system.
  5. Maintain a safe work environment by following established safety operating procedures; safely use equipment provided including forklift, pallet jack, karts, and ladders.
  6. Addresses for Shipments or return shipments must be detailed and accurate.
  7. Accurately assist in completing records, logs, manifests, time sheets and other pertinent documentation to support the jobs function.
  8. Assist inventory or production managers in prepping materials for production and periodic inventory checks when received.
  9. Prompt consistent attendance is required for this position.
  10. Address issues and problems in coordination with the supervisor as necessary.

Requirements:

  1. Able to quickly adapt to new shipping and receiving software
  2. Computer competency is required including average to high level typing speed and proficiency using a web browser.
  3. Experience with forklift, pallet jack, 1 year or more.
  4. Considerable physical activity including handling of objects that can weigh up to 50 lbs.
  5. Work is normally performed in a warehouse and manufacturing environment.
  6. Experience with ocean freight and basic logistics preferred

Skills, Certifications, and Education:

  1. Advanced Data Entry
  2. Previous Forklift certification preferred
  3. GED/ High School Diploma
