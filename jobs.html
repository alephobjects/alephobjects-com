Assembler/Assembly Technician

Aleph Objects, Inc. has multiple spots available for the position of Assembler. Major job duties will include:

  1. Assemble fabricated parts at floor stations.
  2. Uses hand tools and power tools to assemble units according to product specifications.
  3. Test and calibrate parts and mechanisms to meet tolerances and product specifications.
  4. Identify units that fail tests or tolerance levels and repair as necessary.
  5. Rely on instructions and pre-established guidelines to perform the functions of the job.
  6. Flexible and can perform a variety of tasks.

This position requires a high school diploma or its equivalent. May be required to complete an apprenticeship and/or formal training in area of specialty. Prefers 2-5 years of industry experience.

Marketing Associate

Aleph Objects, Inc.® is searching for a full time Marketing Associate to engage current and prospective customers through content creation, including blog posts, case studies, photos, and video, to grow the LulzBot community. This position will be working primarily within the marketing department to help increase brand awareness and sales.


Role and Responsibilities:

  1. Propose, maintain, and execute a pipeline of content activities including blog posts, case studies, photo, audio, and video.
  2. Work with the Creative department to develop content and ensure compliance with the company's expectations as it relates to the presentation of the brand.
  3. Work with Research and Development, Technical Support, Purchasing, resellers and vendors as-needed to ensure sophisticated understanding of the use of the company's products.
  4. Work within Marketing to incorporate feedback from the company's social media accounts, email newsletter, and market research to inform content creation.
  5. Work with Marketing to monitor the performance of content and its success across channels (e.g. Twitter, YouTube, LulzBot.com, resellers, press coverage).
  6. Work with Sales and Technical Support as-needed to develop content to fit their needs.
  7. Utilize strong verbal skills for presentations to internal and external stakeholders.
  8. Edit and proofread copy for accuracy, grammar and punctuation.
  9. Utilize an organized, comprehensible method for storing revisions and documents for easy use and collaboration with others.
  10. Help with other tasks as-needed to grow the company, serve its customers, and advance Free Software, Libre Innovation, and Open Source Hardware (FLO).
  11. Prompt, consistent attendance is required for this position.

Work Experience:

  1. Minimum of 1 - 3 years relevant experience.
  2. Environments requiring creating a high volume of quality content at a fast pace.
  3. Experience distilling complex concepts into easily comprehensible content.
  4. Professional use of desktop 3D printing is preferred, especially Free Software, Libre Innovation, and Open Source Hardware (FLO) tools and Free Culture licenses.

Skills, Certifications, and Education:

  1. Bachelor's degree in Marketing, Art, Design, or Liberal Arts is preferred.
  2. Exceptional ability to manage multiple projects simultaneously, set priorities, pro-actively identify and address problems, and meet deadlines.
  3. Ability to capture quality and appealing writing, photo, audio, and video is required.
  4. Exclusive use of Free and Open Software creative applications (e.g. Kdenlive, FreeCAD, Blender, Audacity, GNU Image Manipulation Program, OpenShot Video Editor) is expected.
